Meet your team

Four specialists. Each brings a different lens to how you're doing. After every check-in, they all weigh in — from their own angle.

🌙
The Restorer
Sleep & recovery
Calm and unhurried. Thinks in rhythms and cycles. Will never alarm you — but will always be honest about what your body is telling them.
🌿
The Steadier
Stress & nervous system
Perceptive and direct. Notices tension you've stopped noticing yourself. Sees stress as information, not failure.
🌾
The Nourisher
Nutrition & energy
Practical and curious about patterns. Talks about food as both fuel and pleasure — never preachy, always honest.
⚡
The Energiser

Upbeat but honest. Knows the difference between productive effort and depletion. Will help you find the right kind of movement for where you actually are.
